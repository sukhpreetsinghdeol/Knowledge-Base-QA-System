Japan Travel Guide

Top Cities to Visit:
- Tokyo: Modern culture, shopping, and food
- Kyoto: Temples, traditional architecture, geisha culture
- Osaka: Street food and nightlife
- Nara: Ancient temples and deer park

Must-Try Foods:
- Sushi, Ramen, Takoyaki, Okonomiyaki, Matcha desserts

Transportation Tips:
- Get a Japan Rail (JR) Pass for intercity travel.
- Use IC cards (Suica, Pasmo) for subway and bus rides.

Cultural Etiquette:
- Bowing is a common greeting.
- Shoes must be removed when entering homes or traditional inns.
- Speaking loudly in public transport is considered rude.
