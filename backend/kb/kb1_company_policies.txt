Company Name: Nexora Technologies

Code of Conduct:
- Employees must adhere to all security and confidentiality agreements.
- Dress code is business casual unless otherwise specified.
- Attendance should be regular; three unexcused absences in a month lead to a warning.

Remote Work Policy:
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week.
- Employees must log in by 9:00 AM and be available on Slack.
- Monthly remote check-ins are required with team leads.

Leave Policy:
- 15 paid vacation days per year.
- 10 sick days annually, which can be carried over to the next year.
- Maternity/Paternity leave as per provincial law.
